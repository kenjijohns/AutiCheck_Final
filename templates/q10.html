Q10/10: Does your child stare at nothing with no apparent purpose?

Example 1: Does your child sometimes seem to fixate on something, like staring at a spot on the wall or into space, without an obvious reason or interest in what's there?

Example 2: Have you noticed instances where your child appears to be lost in thought or fixated on a particular point, perhaps gazing into space or at an object, without any discernible reason or clear focus on what captivates their attention?
